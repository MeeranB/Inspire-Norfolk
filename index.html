Our Services

At Inspire Norfolk, we have numerous different types of apprentice opportunities, meaning we can guarantee you get the apprenticeship qualifications you want in the right industry. Find out further details below.

Apprentice Recruitment

We support businesses every step of the way when recruiting apprentices.

International Work Placements

Students from a range of countries stay in Norwich to complete work placements in a variety of sectors.

Bespoke Support

Individually tailored support for those applying for work or who are already in work but may be struggling.

Working in partnership with Norfolk County Council since 2012

We support international students coming to Norwich on work placements. These placements are with a variety of local companies across a range of sectors including retail, business, the trades and architectural services. Each year this project grows and will in the future include our young people going out to France and Norway, so watch this space.
